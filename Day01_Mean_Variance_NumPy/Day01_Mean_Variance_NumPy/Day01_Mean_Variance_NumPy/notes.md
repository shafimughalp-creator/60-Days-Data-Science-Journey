
# Day 1 — Mean, Variance & NumPy

## Concepts Learned

Mean:
The average value of a dataset.

Formula:
Mean = Sum of values / Number of values

Variance:
Variance measures how far data points spread from the mean.

Low variance → data points close to mean  
High variance → data points far from mean

## Key Insights

If data is multiplied by k:

Mean → multiplied by k  
Variance → multiplied by k²
